# VEX Experience Bible

## Purpose

This document translates elite 2025 portfolio-quality interaction principles into a VEX-native luxury automotive language.

## Creative North Star

- Cinematic first impression within 3 seconds.
- Narrative-driven scroll progression with intentional chapter beats.
- Minimal but expressive interface chrome.
- Dark-luxury atmosphere with metallic highlights and restrained motion.

## Originality Guardrails

- Use interaction archetypes, not direct visual cloning.
- Keep VEX-specific copy, IA, and conversion goals.
- Preserve VEX brand motifs: exchange trust, verified history, white-glove delivery, build-to-order precision.

## Narrative Arc (Homepage)

1. Arrival: universe-scale hero, calm but premium motion.
2. Curation: inventory as collectible objects, not plain cards.
3. Craft: configurator as a luxury instrument.
4. Confidence: logistics, financing, and trust story.
5. Conversion: clear high-intent CTAs without breaking immersion.

## Motion System Principles

- Motion has semantic purpose: orient, reveal, confirm, delight.
- Prefer soft ease curves and stagger for hierarchy.
- Use scroll velocity smoothing to avoid abrupt transitions.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` globally.

## Typography + Rhythm

- Display typography for storytelling headings.
- Clean geometric sans for data and actions.
- Long-form spacing with editorial whitespace, dense only where operationally required.

## Interaction Rules

- Hover states: subtle glow/lift, never noisy.
- Navigation transitions: elegant and legible, not theatrical.
- 3D camera shifts should feel intentional and guided.
- Each section must support keyboard and reduced-motion paths.

## Performance Rules

- Scene-aware effects; no unnecessary always-on heavy layers.
- Adaptive quality tiers for WebGL and expensive filters.
- Minimize cumulative CPU/GPU pressure across stacked effects.

## Acceptance Checklist

- First viewport communicates exclusivity and clarity.
- Scroll flow reads as a story, not a list of blocks.
- Mobile keeps the same narrative intent with reduced complexity.
- Conversion CTAs remain visible and contextually placed.
